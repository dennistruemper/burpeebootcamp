💡 Install as App

📱 Install on iPhone (iOS)
  1. Open BurpeeBootcamp in Safari
  2. Tap the share button ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right

You'll get a fullscreen app experience and quick access from your home screen!

🤖 Install on Android
  1. Open BurpeeBootcamp in Chrome
  2. Tap the three dots menu ⋮ in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm

Enjoy a native app-like experience with home screen access!
